WELCOME TO THE GAMESHOW OF MADNESS

Here's a breakdown of how it'll work to make it easy to understand since y'all be playing too much and asking stupid questions:

Each cabin will choose 3 representatives. There will be 3 different live gameshows in call (hosted at different times ofc) consisting of 20 questions each that will need to be answered buzzer style. (We will use a website for this). The questions consist of audio, visual, and text based questions that test general knowledge. These are a lot easier than the live trivia quiz question, but the challenge comes being the first to buzz in and answer correctly. If you take longer than 2-3 seconds to answer after buzzing in or get the answer incorrect, the second fastest buzzer will get to answer next and so on and so forth. If everyone somehow gets the question wrong, we'll move on to the next one and no one gets a point for that question.

Placements will be decided by OVERALL performance based on cumulative points of every participant in each cabin. (EX: Cabin 1 participants get 7, 6, and 4 correct respectively leading to a score of 17.)
If you get 1st in your individual game show session, you will get a bonus of 20 points. This is so if your teammates tanks the rest of your team in other sessions, you at least won't consider going out with honor.Â 

1st place will be awarded 150 points
2nd place will be awarded 100 points
3rd place will be awarded 50 points
*20 points for getting 1st in your individual gameshow session
