Smash Tournament



SMASH camp. Super SMASH Bros tournament. One of the most longstanding events at camp, you already know what's up. 5th year of the event. Seemed like those who participated last year enjoyed having more fun with the tournament as opposed to tryharding it, so I cooked up a similar ruleset for this year, with an exception. I'm going to add an additional rule this year to hopefully make it more fun for everyone called the Gentleman clause. Your game vs your opponent can have any rules you want that are allowed in battle arenas, so long as you agree on a ruleset. This could be a hardcore competitive ruleset, you could play on great cave offensive, you could let your amiibos battle it out, I don't care as long as you agree. If you cannot agree, then you will play with the base ruleset. Have fun with it remember it's not about winning because if you don't then the wifi will probably ruin your fun :(
Smash Tournament Bracket

NOTE:

Let it be known since Smash Camp is only a week long, you will have to be fairly active each day to consider signing up for this tourney. We don’t want to have to DQ people because they aren't available or whatever, so we just wanna make it clear ONLY sign up if you're sure you can be around for at least an hour or two throughout the day every day.

Rules

Style: Stock
Stock: 3
Time Limit: 10 Minutes (Max allowed in a battle arena)
FS Meter: Off
Spirits: Off
Items: On medium
BANNED ITEMS: Dragoon Parts, Daybreak Parts, All healing items except Food, Clock, Gust Bellows, Beetle, Special Flag
Stage Hazards: On
Stage Morph: Off
Best of 3 (Best of 5 Finals and 3rd place match)


Stage Selection: 1-2-1 striking, Winner bans 2 stages
Starters: Halberd, Kalos Pokemon League, Kongo Jungle 64, Midgar, Pictochat 2
Counterpicks: Cloud Sea of Alrest, Mementos, Super Mario Maker


Scoring

1st place will be awarded 200 points
2nd place will be awarded 150 points
3rd place will be awarded 100 points
4th place will be awarded 50 points
5th place will be awarded 25 points
