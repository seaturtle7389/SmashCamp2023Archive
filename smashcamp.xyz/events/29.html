Pokémon Showdown! Tournament


Pokemon Showdown back at it again for the umpteenth Smash Camp in a row. Smash Camp has been running pokemon since Gen 5 with Pokemon Black and White, all the way back on the old simulator Pokemon Online in 2012. This will be the 12th edition of the event! For the first Gen 9 tournament in Smash Camp history, we're keeping it simple and doing OU. The semi-recent release of Pokemon Home compatability with Pokemon Scarlet and Violet has shaken up the meta into something interesting by adding in powerful Hisuian pokemon, older legendaries, and a couple other returning favorites. With these shifts and the ability to Terastallize your pokemon, anyone could win this tournament - sign up so it can be you!
Showdown Tournament Bracket

NOTE:

Let it be known since Smash Camp is only a week long, you will have to be fairly active each day to consider signing up for this tourney. We don’t want to have to DQ people because they aren't available or whatever, so we just wanna make it clear ONLY sign up if you're sure you can be around for at least an hour or two throughout the day every day.
