Smash Camp Bingo

Campers, I welcome you to Smash Camp Bingo! A concept fantasized for years and finally birthed during the finale of Smash Camp. Smash Camp Bingo is going to be a unique event this year that will test the true skill and coordination of a whole cabin while allowing each member to individually show off their contributions. What you are tasked with is completing as many tiles as possible of a bingo board of 25 tasks. Each task will give you a random number of points not revealed till the end. Completing a bingo row horizontally, vertically, or diagonally will give you 15 bonus points. For the sake of equality, all cabins will receive the same individual points for a particular challenge and will all be using the same board provided above.

The exciting part is that only one member of each cabin needs to complete a challenge to mark off the tile for the cabin. It’s essential for teams to coordinate and show valid proof that each challenge was completed DURING THIS SMASH CAMP. Specifics for each challenge will be down below but a general note of showing a paper with your username or a video/picture of yourself and/or the time will be essential. In short: please use common sense to verify some of the challenges depending on what is asked. Further clarification should be asked about in the public questions channel.

The scoring is as follows:

Up to 625 points for completing the whole board with a RANDOM distribution of the following points per tile:
18, 19, 20, 20, 20, 21, 22, 22, 23, 23, 24, 24, 24, 25, 25, 26, 27, 28, 29, 29, 30, 30, 31, 32, 33

Additionally there is up to 180 points in bonuses for completing twelve bingos for a possible total of 805 points!!!!!
