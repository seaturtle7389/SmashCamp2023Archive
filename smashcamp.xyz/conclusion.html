And now, without further ado..... Here are the cabin rankings for Smash Camp 2023!


Camp's Unrivaled Newly Trained Superidols (CUNTS): 4193

The CUNTS started off the camp quite rough but here at the tail end of the camp they really pulled off a massive comeback. Newcomers SilverCyanide and Eiscrack, as well as veterans Duck, Zange, and Elmo, really pulled up for their cabin in many different events. Although they may be last, they were certainly no Positivity Cabin and should be proud of one hell of a comeback they made on Day Five.

Final Adventure Road Trip (F.A.R.T.): 4269

Cabin 2 finished third in the final Smash Camp. The team got off to a hot start under their leader, piplup17, and with big performances from members like Gordo, Calaf, and hotguactony they looked to be a forced to be reckoned with. Unfortunately for them, this hot start didn't last long despite stellar bingo and trivia performances as this cabin began to stink it up in tournaments and side events throughout the week. Cabin 2 continued to fight proudly till the end with help from Catball Enthusiast and Saffiya, but alas, they just ran out of gas.

W Cabin W Cabin: 4416

This year's runner-up cabin is W Cabin W Cabin, who were strong all camp but didn't quite get the W they were searching for. The core group of Bulbajohn and Big Dan brought home high scores and tournament victories as expected as W Cabin W Cabin jumped out to a lead early on in camp. High performance from Ritvik in tournaments and Pastel in art contributed more points to the cabin. They turned in solid performances all around for the duration of camp but weren't able to lock down enough of the events towards the end. First year cabin leader Rex led this group to a solid 2nd place to a worthy opponent. The W Cabin W Cabin might be going home with an L, but the real W was the friends they made along the way.

Peculiar Dogs: 4940

The winner of Smash Camp 2023 is The Peculiar Dogs, and the point total by the end didn't look close. Cabin leader Hot Bread brought energy to the dogs as they were packed in call all camp long providing support to each other and enjoying their time. Hot Bread's squad of Coal Soul and Spos3 plus contributors like BB flooded these calls all week. Cabin 4 were high in the scores from the beginning thanks to Pesti and Brian's performance on flash games, and winning big in weekly events to close out camp widened the gap between them and the rest. The Peculiar Dogs were truly a different breed, and have rightfully earned the title of Smash Camp 2023 Champion! THE CURSE IS BROKEN!

From us here at Smash Camp, we only have one thing left to say:

Happy trails to you all, 'till we meet again.
