Medley of Madness

A Smash Camp tradition - our leaders and crew have compiled an evil guess-the-song competition! Timestamps of each song have been provided on the page linked below. The songs are from mixed genres, including but most definitely not limited to pop, alternative, rock, video games, and anime.

Click HERE to listen to the Medley of Madness!

Contest Guidelines

  • As a cabin, complete the list of songs that you can guess to the best of your ability.
  • YOU MAY NOT GOOGLE LYRICS OR USE AUDIO-DETECTING SOFTWARE. You can, however, Google a song you think might be the one you're listening to.
  • This challenge is due on Wednesday at 11:59 EDT. Counselors, please use the Google Doc for the medley found in your cabin folders to use as the submission.
  • Fill out your guesses in "Artist - Song" format. If it is in any other format, the guess will not count. Our sanity thanks you for your compliance.

When adding guesses to your cabin's Google Doc, you MUST include your name alongside your contributions.


2 points will be awarded for each correct title and artist/game/anime/creator named. 1 point will be awarded if only the correct title or artist/game/anime/creator is named.

Additionally, cabin performance will be awarded with placement bonuses.
1st place will be awarded 200 points
2nd place will be awarded 100 points
3rd place will be awarded 50 points

 

Tiktok

Create a TikTok-style video! Options range from funny skits to speedarts - anything you can think of in 60 seconds or less! Make us laugh or amaze us - it's your choice. The steps are simple: record a thing on your phone, submit it, and hope you get points.

Contest Guidelines

  • The video must be taken vertically, just as if it were in TikTok itself.
  • You don't actually have to face reveal or post this on social media.
  • You may use any type of software to edit your video.
  • To submit, upload as a TikTok and post the link OR upload as an unlisted video on YouTube, Dropbox, Google Drive, etc. Provide the link in your submissions channel.
  • There will be no participation points for this event. Failure to follow the time or phone orientation limit will result in disqualification from the leaderboard.
  • No NSFW content or slurs allowed.

1st place will be awarded 100 points
2nd place will be awarded 75 points
3rd place will be awarded 50 points
4th and 5th place will be awarded 25 points
6th through 10th place will be awarded 10 points

Scavenger Hunt (Day 3)

A staple of Smash Camp that we're excited to bring back this year! For this event, we will provide a list of items that each camper will need to find and take a picture of in real life. Find them, take a picture of them (with the required proof - see below), and get some points! You will earn 5 points for each picture submitted, with an extra 5 bonus points available for specific items.

Contest Guidelines

  • You may only take photos of these items in person (in your house, at a store, wherever as long as you're physically with the item). It cannot be a screenshot or an image from the internet.
  • Your photo absolutely must be accompanied by a slip of paper with your username and cabin number on it.
  • In the past we've been lenient with the validity of submissions that were not totally accurate but showed effort and/or creativity. This year, we will only accept submissions that are accurate as to what we're requesting (e.g. if we ask for a lime, we wouldn't accept a lemon that's been painted green).
  • Bonus points will be available for one or more items per day. These points will be awarded at the discretion of camp staff for submissions that meet the bonus requirements.

Today's items are the following:
  • A flip phone or corded telephone Bonus if Nokia
  • A physical copy of a PC game Bonus if game was made before 2000
  • An analog clock (watches/digital clocks do NOT count) Bonus if grandfather clock

Cluezle (Day 3)

We will be giving you five things (character, item, place, etc) to guess as a cabin through the use of clues we provide. We will all be creating a thread/forum post in your cabin starting you off with one clue at first. You guys will have to make a collective guess as a team. If you guess wrong, we will provide you an additional hint and will keep going till all five hints have been given. You will be given points based on how many hints it took for you to get the right answer. 

Getting the correct answer on the 1st guess will award 25 points
Getting the correct answer on the 2nd guess will award 20 points
Getting the correct answer on the 3rd guess will award 15 points
Getting the correct answer on the 4th guess will award 10 points
Getting the correct answer on the 5th guess will award 5 points
